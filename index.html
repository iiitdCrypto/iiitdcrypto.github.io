सममित गूढलेखन प्रयोगशाला

The Symmetric Cryptography Lab focuses on the design and security analysis of symmetric cryptographic primitives. Our research explores the structural properties of block and stream ciphers, with an emphasis on developing cryptanalytic techniques.

We also study quantum cryptanalysis to understand the impact of quantum adversaries on symmetric-key security. Recently, we have started exploring the application of machine learning methods to cryptanalysis.

Research Areas

Classical Cryptanalysis
Quantum Cryptanalysis
Design of Symmetric Ciphers
Machine Learning for Cryptanalysis
